## 1. Spirit of the agreement
This is a consensual, private arrangement entered into freely by both parties. It is built on trust, affection, and honesty. It may be paused, modified or terminated by either party at any time without cause.

## 2. Roles
- The **Dominant** sets the terms of the session — tasks, rewards, redemptions, milestones, and targets.
- The **submissive** accepts and performs the agreed tasks in good faith. She does not set her own points, targets, or rewards.

- Points are tracked in the private *Good Girl Points* portal.
- The submissive may *claim* points for something done, or *offer* to do something to earn points. Every request is reviewed by the Dominant, who may approve, amend, or decline.
- Points may only be spent on rewards in the catalogue set by the Dominant.

- Hard limits agreed in advance override any task, reward, or decision.

## 5. Honesty
Both parties agree to honesty above all. False claims, hidden redemptions, or dishonest submissions void the session and forfeit any current balance.

## 6. Privacy
Everything exchanged under this agreement is private. Neither party shares portal access, screenshots, or session details with third parties without written consent of the other.

## 7. End of session
At the agreed end time, the final balance is settled: any outstanding approved points may be spent on one final redemption at the Dominant's discretion, or banked into the next session by mutual agreement.

## 8. Aftercare
The Dominant undertakes to check in with the submissive at the end of every session, regardless of outcome, and to offer care, conversation, and closure.
